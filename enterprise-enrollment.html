More ways to enroll

Zero-touch isn’t the only way to enroll. If it’s not available where you are, Android offers other simple options to get your devices set up.

Near-field communication

Set up a new device by bumping an NFC tag.

EMM token

Enter the unique code provided by your EMM provider.

QR code

Enroll a device from the setup wizard by easily scanning a QR code.

Enterprise Google account

Simply sign in to enroll with a work email address on Google Workspace.
